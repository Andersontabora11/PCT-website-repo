The Zeta Nu Chapter

Founded on December 8, 2013, the Zeta Nu Chapter at The Ohio State University is the premier co‑ed, professional business fraternity on campus. Built on four pillars—Professionalism, Philanthropy, Diversity, Equity & Inclusion (DEI), and Brotherhood—we aim to empower every member academically, professionally, and personally.

What ultimately sets Phi Chi Theta apart is the consistent structure of professional and personal development and our lifelong commitment to supporting our members and maximizing their impact in the world.

As a Phichi, you’ll have access to events, conferences, resources, and connections to build your professional skills, leadership abilities, and character.

Membership in our brotherhood grants you access to a network that spans virtually every industry, and opportunities to teach you how to succeed. Most importantly, you gain the strength of brotherhood that lasts not solely during your college career, but for life.

That’s what makes PCT Fisher’s Phinest Professional Business Fraternity.
