A Presidential Welcome!

Hi there—I’m Wyatt Greenlees, President of the Zeta Nu chapter at The Ohio State University.

Phi Chi Theta is more than a business fraternity; it’s a community of ambitious, co-ed leaders united by our pillars of brotherhood, professionalism, and service. As members from diverse majors and career goals, we share a growth mindset: we want to lead, learn, and lift each other.

Joining this organization has transformed my college experience—I’ve gained mentors and mentees, developed confidence in high-pressure settings, and built lasting friendships that push me to be the best version of myself.

Whether you’re a student curious about rush, an alum reconnecting, or a company exploring partnerships, I hope Phi Chi Theta can change your life the way it’s changed mine. Welcome—and thanks for stopping by!

Wyatt Greenlees | greenlees.10@osu.edu
