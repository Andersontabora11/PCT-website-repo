DIVERSITY, EQUITY,
& INCLUSION

Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion: As the first business fraternity to incorporate DE&I as a pillar, we are committed to creating a welcoming and supportive community. By embracing our diverse perspectives and backgrounds and celebrating our differences, we foster an environment where every member feels valued and strive to continuously learn and grow alongside each other.
